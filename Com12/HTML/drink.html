Ingredients

Tea 2 Large Mason Jars about 4-5 cups each, filled with water, 4 Green Tea Bags two in each jar, Bubbles 1/2 Cup Large Black Tapioca Pearls, Peach Purée 2 Peaches pitted and peeled, 1/4 Cup Maple Syrup 1/4 Cup Water For Serving Almond or other type of plant milk Maple Syrup to taste.

How To Make It

Tea Start by making your tea. There's two methods you can use - the standard and solar tea methods. If making standard tea, bring the water to a boil, then let cool for a minute. (Adding tea to boiling water can make it bitter.) Add the hot water to mason jars (or any heat-proof vessel), then add two tea bags for each jar to steep for two minutes. When done steeping, remove tea bags. If you want to make solar tea, add the water to the mason jars at room temperature, then add two tea bags per each jar. Put the jars of tea out into the sun and let steep for 3-4 hours. Once tea is done (using either method), chill in the refrigerator for an hour or two. Or, just add ice and shake it up in the jars. Bubbles Cook 1/2 cup tapioca pearls according to package directions. When done, drain and set aside to cool. Peach Purée In a blender, combine peeled peaches, maple syrup, and water. Blend until smooth. Putting it all Together Add half of the peach purée to one mason jar, and the other half in the other mason jar. Close the lids and shake the tea very well in each jar. Taste, and adjust if needed - i.e., add maple syrup. When ready to serve, add as many tapioca peals into your glass as you like. Fill the glasses with tea, leaving room for plant milk if desired (I used almond milk). Enjoy!
