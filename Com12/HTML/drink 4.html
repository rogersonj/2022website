Ingredients

30mL SHOTT Vanilla 50g Ice 40g Tapioca Pearls 180mL Full Cream Milk

How To Make It

Add the tapioca pearls and ice to a cup. Pour the SHOT Vanilla over the pearls and ice. Top with the milk and stir to combine. Serve & enjoy.
